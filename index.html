What is Global Warming/ Climate Change?

In simple terms, Global Warming is the increase of heating in earth's climate. There are a lot of factors that contribute to global warming. However the factor we have the most control over is the emission of Greenhouse gases. These gases get trapped in Earth's atmosphere which result in temperature increase. This increase in temperature is substantial because it can lead to extreme weather conditions, natural disasters, rising sea levels and the loss of certain species. Resources to better accquaint yourself about greenhouse gases and climate change are under our learn more page.

What is Eco-Anxiety?

Eco-Anxiety is the feeling of fear for the current state of the environment and the fear of environmental doom. This fear is completely rational as these events may disturb the state of our comfortable lives on Earth. As opposed to ecological greif, having eco-anxiety doesn't mean that you've accepted that the crisis is inevitable or that you have given up. Eco-anxiety can be your motivation to rise up and make a change!
